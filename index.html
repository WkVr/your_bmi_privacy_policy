Your BMI App - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024/01/16

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No User Data Collected

Your BMI app does not collect any personal information from users. We respect your privacy, and our app is designed to operate without the need for gathering personal data.

1.2 Physical Appearance Information

While using Your BMI, you may be prompted to provide information related to your physical appearance. This information is used solely within the app and is not stored or shared with any third parties.

2. User Authentication

Your BMI does not require users to create an account or log in to use its features. We believe in providing a seamless and private experience without the need for unnecessary personal information.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected regarding physical appearance is used exclusively within the app for the purpose of calculating and displaying BMI. This data is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with external parties.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Your BMI employs industry-standard measures to protect the information provided by users. However, as no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at wykovr@gmail.com.

By using Your BMI, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
